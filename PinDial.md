# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-08

This Privacy Policy explains how PinDial: Sales Route CRM ("the App") handles information when you use it.

## Summary

- The App does **not** read your system contacts, call logs, SMS, or background location.
- Lead data you create in the App is stored **locally** on your device in **encrypted** storage.
- You can export/import an **encrypted** backup file from the Settings screen.
- You can export a CSV file of your leads from the App, but CSV exports are **not encrypted**.
- Pro Solo (optional, via Google Play in-app purchase) unlocks paid workflow features.
- Reverse geocoding is optional; when enabled, selected coordinates may be sent to a backend proxy and then to Google or another provider to resolve country/region names.
- Some builds may also enable optional account, purchase verification, or sync features.

## Information you provide

When you create or edit a lead, route stop, or follow-up record, you may provide:

- Name
- Phone number
- Notes and visit notes
- Tags and status
- Follow-up dates
- Map locations you select manually (latitude/longitude)
- Optional region fields derived from reverse geocoding

## How the App uses this information

The App uses this information to:

- Display leads on the map
- Search leads around a selected center point and radius
- Build and reorder route-day stops
- Log visit outcomes and next follow-ups
- Open your phone dialer using `ACTION_DIAL` when you tap "Call"
- Export backups, CSV files, or encrypted lead cards when you request them
- Unlock and restore paid features

## In-app purchases

If you choose to buy Pro Solo, the purchase is processed by Google Play Billing. The App may query purchase status on your device to unlock paid features and restore purchases.

We do not receive or store your payment card details.

## What the App does not access

The App does not request or access:

- System contacts
- Call logs
- SMS messages
- Background location tracking
- Automatic phone calling

The App uses the system dialer so that you confirm calls yourself.

## Third-party services

The App may use third-party services for core functionality:

- Google Maps SDK for Android
- Google Play Billing
- Reverse geocoding providers (only if enabled)
- Firebase services for optional account, verification, or sync features in supported builds

These providers may process standard request metadata such as IP address as part of delivering their services.

## Reverse geocoding (optional)

If reverse geocoding is enabled in your build/configuration, the App may send the coordinates of a lead to:

1) a backend proxy you control, and then
2) Google Geocoding API or another configured provider,

to obtain country/administrative area information for display.

## Storage and retention

- Local storage: lead data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- Exported files: encrypted backups and encrypted lead cards remain wherever you save them until you delete them.
- CSV exports are plain files and should be handled carefully.
- If optional sync features are enabled and used, encrypted remote vaults may remain in the selected cloud destination until deleted.

## Your choices

You can:

- Delete individual leads
- Clear all local leads from the App
- Export/import encrypted backups
- Export CSV files when available
- Delete your account in-app if account features are enabled and exposed in production

## Security

We use:

- Encryption at rest for local storage
- Password-based encryption for exported backups and encrypted lead cards
- HTTPS for configured network requests

No storage or transmission method is 100% secure.

## Children

The App is not directed to children under 13.
